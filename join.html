PHD/MASTER STUDENTS

Requirements

We are looking for highly motivated PhD and master students to conduct world-class research in our team. Here are some requirements:

  • Highly motivated and interests in deep learning and machine learning.
  • Solid mathematical skills and programming skills.
  • Good English reading and writing skills.
  • Enjoys scientific research and has strong stress resilience.
  • For PhD students, published at least one high-quality academic paper.

Application

Please send the following materials to the email address juana.alian@gmail.com with the subject line indicating: PhD/Master's Application + Your Name,

  • Resume in both Chinese and English (including school and major ranking).
  • Undergraduate (and Master's) transcripts.
  • Other materials that can help us learn more about you (such as competition experience, etc.)
  • If you have published articles, please attach the PDF files of the articles.
For qualified candidates, we will notify you for a video interview.

UNDERGRADUATE STUDENTS

Requirements

We are looking for promising undergraduate students to join our team for research training. Students who are interested in research are welcome to contact us. Here are some requirements:

  • Highly motivated and interests in deep learning and machine learning.
  • Good programming skills.
  • At least one-third of the work time can be dedicated to research.

Application

Please send the following materials to the email address juana.alian@gmail.com with the subject line indicating: Undergraduate Students Research's Application + Your Name,

  • Resume in Chinese.
  • Undergraduate (and Master's) transcripts.
  • Other materials that can help us learn more about you (such as research experience, competition experience, etc.)

For qualified candidates, we will notify you for a video interview.

Note

We follow a project-based research system and will assign mentors to undergraduate students. All students participating in research training need to select an appropriate topic within two weeks. The topic selection is considered successful once the mentor confirms your choice and agrees to guide you through the research Afterward, you will conduct corresponding research on the selected topic. If the research yields significant results, we will assist you in submitting a paper for publication.

Students are required to discuss and report their research progress with their mentor at least once a week. If there are repeated instances of idleness without a valid reason, the research training will be terminated.
