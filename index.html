About Us

VLLab@UESTC was formed on the 1 August 2017, with a research focus on computer vision and deep learning. It is now a group with two faculty members and more than 50 members including research fellows, PhD students and master students.

Members in VLLab@UESTC conduct research primarily in generative AI, human-centric understanding, cross-modal understanding, adversarial attack, compact representation, agent and embodied AI. Have a look at the overview of our research. Selected publications are listed here.

We are always looking for motivated PhD students, postdocs, research assistants who have the same interests like us.
